# Plan 10-02 - Wrapped Selection Geometry and Highlight Rendering

## Objective

Map pointer positions into wrapped single-node text ranges and render the selected range with dedicated theme tokens while leaving non-selected shell content untouched.

## Design Truths

- D-06: Wrapped text inside one selectable node is part of Phase 10 and should behave naturally across visual lines.
- D-07: Clipped or ellipsized text remains excluded from Phase 10 selection.
- D-09: Phase 10 introduces `color.selection-background` and `color.selection-foreground`.
- D-10: Selected state fully overrides the node's ordinary passive text colors while selected.

<threat_model>
T-10-02: Selection geometry and paint could disagree about visible text, producing wrong copied ranges, broken highlights, or hidden-text selection.
Mitigation: Build geometry and paint on the same `cosmic-text` layout helper, reject ellipsized/clipped targets early, and add focused render tests for wrapped lines and multi-byte text.
</threat_model>
