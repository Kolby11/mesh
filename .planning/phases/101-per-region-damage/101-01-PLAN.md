<objective>
Thread `Vec<DamageRect>` end-to-end from the component paint layer through the shell render dispatch and presentation engine into per-rect `wl_surface::damage_buffer` calls, replacing the single unioned `Option<DamageRect>` currently committed every frame.

Purpose: The compositor currently receives one bounding rect covering the union of all dirty regions, forcing it to re-composit unchanged pixels. Sending per-region damage lets the compositor scan only changed regions (DMGE-01, DMGE-02).

Output: A single atomic, compilable refactor across six integration points: the `ShellComponent` trait, `FrontendSurfaceComponent` storage + paint + take, the render dispatch loop, `PresentationEngine::present_with_damage`, and the Wayland backend's `present_with_damage` / `copy_into_shm_buffer` / `attach_shm_buffer`. Plus new presentation-layer unit tests proving the per-rect loop and the 16-cap union fallback.
</objective>
