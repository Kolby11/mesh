# Plan 15-04 - Attribution Snapshot Proof and Phase Verification

## Objective

Finalize regression proof and verification guidance so Phase 15 closes with explicit evidence for per-surface timing breakdowns and backend provider/service stage attribution.

## Design Truths

- The phase closes on shell-owned evidence, not manual interpretation.
- Verification must exercise backend and surface attribution together.
- Disabled-mode silence remains part of the acceptance contract.

</threat_model>
